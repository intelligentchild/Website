Welcome to LAXMI NARAYAN TRUST

For people having mentally challenged children, it is their inbuilt fear as to how and who would provide care to such child in the event of incapacity of the parent owing to failing health or eventual death. To ease this concern that these ageing parents may have about the future care of their child , we, as a service provider take care of learning , psychological, emotional, social, medical and behavioural need of these inmates.
